In an ideal world all the needs of a company would be met in one system, however that is not realistic regardless of the size of a company. Making disparate systems communicate with one another and passing information between those systems is critical. To leverage various smart and necessary solutions, integration between those systems is paramount to the success of a business. Glacier Systems has the expertise to make multiple systems effectively communicate. For example, making your ERP system share data with Dynamics 365 CRM.
