Dynamics 365 CRM

CRM Installation and Implementation

In general CRM and ERP systems have big footprints and require expert level knowledge of the solutions to implement them. Dynamics 365 CRM also known as Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement (CE) will require good understanding of the functionality as well as technical details before implementation. It is critical to start on the right track when it comes to systems like this. Glacier Systems' consultants can help you navigate through each decision making step and help in its implementation.

CRM Integration

In an ideal world all the needs of a company would be in one system but that is not realistic regardless of the size of a company. Making disparate systems communicate with one another and passing information between those systems is a must. To leverage various smart and necessary solutions, integration between those systems is paramount to the success of a business. Glacier Systems has the expertise to make those systems communicate. For example, making your ERP system share data with Dynamics 365 CRM.

CRM Plugins and Web Resource Development

Regardless of how sophisticated a business solution is, one system does not fit for different businesses. A majority of functionality needed to manage sales cycle is in Dynamics 365 but if your business has a unique process that the system does not handle, some form of customization will be necessary. Our team is well versed in solving your business process needs leveraging various Power Platform with limited coding. However, if hardcore development is needed to meet the business need, we have expertise to create plugins, web services, client side customization or complex cloud flows.

CRM Data Handling and Migration Services

Software as a service (SaaS) is mainstream at this point and Dynamics 365 CRM is mature SaaS solution. Still there is a need to understand the data and work with the data that that Dynamics 365 CRM maintains. There are situations where we need to bring in the data from older systems into the new implementation. Our team has a deep knowledge on how to work with the data coming from outside or within the system.

Support and Maintenance

Once all the implementation is in place, it needs to be supported in an on-going basis. It is more cost effective to be supported by our team for more reasonable cost. Our team is spread across multiple continents to give 24/7 coverage if there is such a need.
